How Metrics Are Calculated

Risk Calculation (1-100%)
  • Price Risk (35% weight): Higher landed cost = higher risk
  • Competition Risk (25% weight): More similar products = higher risk
  • Seller Risk (20% weight): Lower seller rating = higher risk
  • Lead Time Risk (15% weight): Longer delivery = higher risk
  • Trend Risk (5% weight): Declining trends = higher risk

Formula: Weighted average of all risk factors

Success Likelihood (0-100%)
  • Base Score: Product score from pipeline analysis
  • Risk Penalty: -40% max for high-risk products
  • Margin Bonus: +20% max for high-profit products
  • Trend Bonus/Penalty: ±15% based on growth trends

Formula: Base Score - Risk Penalty + Margin Bonus + Trend Bonus
